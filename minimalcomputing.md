# Minimal Computing

### Overview

This page provides an overview of Minimal Computing tools and techniques for research and data analysis in low-bandwidth, low-resource environments, ideal for fieldwork and remote areas with limited internet and computational resources.

### Disciplines and Techniques

#### Anthropology
- **Cultural Anthropology:** Ethnographic fieldwork, participant observation, interviews.
  - **Tools:** Audio recorders, cameras.
  - **Storage:** External hard drives, local servers.
  - **Software:** NVivo, ATLAS.ti.
  - **Statistical Techniques:** SPSS, R.

- **Archaeology:** Excavations, artifact analysis, radiocarbon dating.
  - **Tools:** Excavation tools.
  - **Storage:** External hard drives, local servers.
  - **Software:** NVivo, ATLAS.ti.
  - **Statistical Techniques:** SPSS, R.

- **Physical Anthropology:** Excavations, skeletal analysis, artifact collection.
  - **Tools:** 3D scanning, photogrammetry.
  - **Storage:** Digital archives, physical storage.
  - **Software:** Morphologika, SPSS.
  - **Statistical Techniques:** Shape analysis.

- **Physical Geography:** Field measurements, remote sensing.
  - **Tools:** Specialized databases, physical sample storage.
  - **Storage:** External hard drives, local servers.
  - **Software:** ArcGIS, QGIS, ENVI.
  - **Statistical Techniques:** Environmental modeling.

#### Environmental Science
- **Techniques:** Sensors and monitors, field surveys.
  - **Tools:** Environmental databases, physical sample storage.
  - **Storage:** External hard drives, local servers.
  - **Software:** Matlab, R, Python.
  - **Statistical Techniques:** Environmental modeling.

- **Botany:** Specimen collection, genetic analysis.
  - **Tools:** Microscopes, DNA sequencers.
  - **Storage:** Specimen databases, genetic data repositories.
  - **Software:** GIS, R, Python.
  - **Statistical Techniques:** Spatial analysis.

#### Social Psychology
- **Techniques:** Experiments, surveys, observational studies.
  - **Tools:** Online survey tools, psychometric tests.
  - **Storage:** Digital databases, encrypted storage.
  - **Software:** Qualtrics, SPSS, R.
  - **Statistical Techniques:** Data analysis.

### Common Resources

- **Open Data Kit (ODK):** Can create a variety of form types, works online and offline, connects to various apps, and is used across various sectors (public health, global development, crisis response, environmental research, etc.). 
- **KoboToolbox:** Open source platform for collecting, managing, and visualizing data. Used by social impact organizations worldwide for research, monitoring, evaluation, and humanitarian action. 
- **Paper:** Forms, questionnaires, surveys.
- **Local storage:** USB drives, external hard drives, local servers.
- **NVivo:** Offline functionality with collaboration cloud.
